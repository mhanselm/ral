About

Scavenger Rally is an adventure race made up of teams of 2-5 members per vehicle, each team needs to complete tasks/challenges to reach the next check point. The scavenger rally follows a designated route that could see you stopping at beaches, pubs, shopping centres, bars, parks, taverns or well known tourist destinations. At each checkpoint teams are issued with a clue to find the next check point, the clue also includes a Primary Challenge / Task that has to be completed before your team reaches the checkpoint.

It's a race, so the team with the most points at the end of the scavenger rally wins!

Points are awarded to the team that arrives first at each check point, bonus points can also be earned by all teams that complete tasks/challenges from the bonus points list. Each team will be issued with a BONUS POINTS LIST at the start of the Scavenger rally; the list contains over 100 possible ways to earn extra points for your team! Your team needs to find creative ways to scavenge as many items on the list to earn extra points. The tasks can be as simple as taking a photo next to a specific road sign for 10 points, to finding a calendar from an Insurance company for 20 points. At each check point your score card will be updated by the rule masters.
